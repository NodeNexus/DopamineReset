⚔ The Four Rites of Installation ⚔

I

Download & Unzip the Artefact

Click the Download the Arcane Extension button above. Once downloaded, locate the dopamine-reset-extension.zip file in your Downloads folder and extract / unzip it into a permanent folder — do not delete this folder after installation.

II

Open the Browser's Extension Vault

Open Google Chrome or Microsoft Edge. In the address bar, type exactly:

chrome://extensions edge://extensions

In the top-right corner of the Extensions page, toggle on Developer mode. This unlocks the ability to load extensions from your local vault.

III

Load the Unpacked Artefact

Click the Load unpacked button that appears after enabling Developer mode. Navigate to the folder you extracted in Step I and select it. The Dopamine Reset extension will appear in your list — make sure its toggle is enabled.

IV
