COMFORT WRAITH - BODYGUARD BACKSTORIES

BodyGuard1 - Mistling Spirit A fragile ghost drifting through quiet
chambers. It forms from sighs of fatigue. Soft whispers gather around
its pale form. It fades before courageous intent.

BodyGuard2 - Hunger Shade A shadow born in dim candlelight kitchens. It
murmurs sweet promises of relief. Yet its hunger is never satisfied.
Restraint causes it to crumble.

BodyGuard3 - Gravebound Whisper An echo from forgotten sorrows. It
glides through stone crypts of avoidance. Each indulgence deepens its
spectral voice. Reflection silences its chant.

BodyGuard4 - Echo Apparition A looping phantom born from repeated
comforts. It lingers where habits repeat. Its presence dulls ambition.
Breaking cycles banishes it.

BodyGuard5 - Pale Mourner Cloaked in soft grey mist, it feeds on
retreat. It mourns growth and change. Those who confront discomfort
weaken its lament. Strength disperses its sorrowful veil.
