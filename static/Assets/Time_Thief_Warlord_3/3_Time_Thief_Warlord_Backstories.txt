TIME THIEF WARLORD - BODYGUARD BACKSTORIES

BodyGuard1 - Clockwork Squire A mechanical servant forged from abandoned
hours. Its chest ticks with stolen minutes. It marches tirelessly
through the ruins of broken schedules. Every wasted second strengthens
its gears.

BodyGuard2 - Temporal Warden An armored sentinel bound by fractured
time. Runes glow across its metal frame. It guards the Warlord’s
dominion over lost days. To defeat it, one must reclaim control.

BodyGuard3 - Chrono Hound A relentless beast formed from collapsing
deadlines. Its metallic claws scratch at slipping time. It hunts
hesitation with ruthless precision. Momentum alone can outrun it.

BodyGuard4 - Rustbound Executioner Encased in corroded steel, it swings
an hour-forged axe. Its blade carries the weight of unfinished tasks. It
appears when resolve falters. Completion is its undoing.

BodyGuard5 - Gearborn Sentinel The Warlord’s most loyal construct.
Powered by engines of endless delay. Its eyes glow with ticking
inevitability. Only disciplined action halts its march.
