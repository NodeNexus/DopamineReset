CRIMSON TEMPTRESS - BODYGUARD BACKSTORIES

BodyGuard1 - Infernal Imp A mischievous spark torn from the Temptress’s
eternal flame. It delights in igniting fleeting desires. With ember-red
skin and glowing eyes, it dances in forbidden warmth. Its laughter
echoes through burning halls.

BodyGuard2 - Ash Succubus Spawn A lesser daughter of seductive flame and
shadow. She glides through crimson smoke with haunting grace. Her
presence blurs the line between strength and surrender. Many knights
have fallen to her whispered promises.

BodyGuard3 - Flame Wraith A spirit born from indulgent firestorms. It
drifts like living flame through obsidian corridors. Its touch leaves
lingering scorch marks upon resolve. It burns brightest in isolation.

BodyGuard4 - Cinder Fiend Carved from volcanic rage and molten
compulsion. Its skin cracks with glowing magma veins. Each defeat
strengthens its furious roar. Only fearless confrontation quenches its
fury.

BodyGuard5 - Hellbound Seraph Once a radiant guardian, now consumed by
crimson corruption. Its shattered wings blaze in infernal anguish. It
guards the Temptress with tragic devotion. Breaking its loyalty demands
unshaken will.
