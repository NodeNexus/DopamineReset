VALIDATION PHANTOM - BODYGUARD BACKSTORIES

BodyGuard1 - Mirror Imp A mischievous creature born from shattered
reflection. Its glassy skin mirrors hidden doubts. It feeds on external
approval. Self-belief cracks its surface.

BodyGuard2 - Reflection Stalker A silent predator lurking within
mirrors. It sharpens itself on comparison. Its blade gleams with envy.
Confidence blinds its vision.

BodyGuard3 - Echo Doppelganger A distorted twin formed from insecurity.
It mimics perceived flaws mercilessly. Comparison breathes life into it.
Gratitude weakens its shadow.

BodyGuard4 - Shardling A floating fragment of fractured esteem. It
scatters attention across endless reflections. Recognition strengthens
its edges. Silence dulls its shine.

BodyGuard5 - Illusion Weaver A cloaked manipulator of distorted
self-image. It threads glamour through false admiration. External praise
fuels its illusions. Inner certainty unravels its spell.
