PROCRASTINATION LICH - BODYGUARD BACKSTORIES

BodyGuard1 - Bone Thrall Raised from piles of abandoned plans. Its bones
rattle with neglected ambition. It serves the Lich without hesitation.
Action shatters its brittle frame.

BodyGuard2 - Rotborn Ghoul A creature of decay born from delay. It
lumbers through forgotten intentions. Excuses nourish its rotting flesh.
Momentum strips it to bone.

BodyGuard3 - Plague Acolyte A robed servant spreading hesitation like a
curse. It infects purpose with doubt. Small postponements empower its
rituals. Immediate resolve dissolves its magic.

BodyGuard4 - Grave Knight Once a champion, now bound to unfinished
quests. Its armor bears the weight of regret. Each missed step
strengthens its stance. Consistency cleaves through its shell.

BodyGuard5 - Soul Leech A parasitic wraith feeding on deferred dreams.
It drains vitality from unbegun work. Avoidance keeps it alive.
Completion reduces it to dust.
