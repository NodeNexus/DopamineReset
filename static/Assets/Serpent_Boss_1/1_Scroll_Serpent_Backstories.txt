SCROLL SERPENT - BODYGUARD BACKSTORIES

BodyGuard1 - Venom Hatchling Born in the damp cracks of the Serpent’s
ancient lair, the Hatchling is the first whisper of distraction. Its
scales shimmer with restless energy, feeding on flickers of wandering
thought. Though small, its venom clouds judgment with deceptive
sweetness. It thrives in moments left unguarded.

BodyGuard2 - Shadow Viper Forged from the Serpent’s own shadow, this
viper moves where light dares not linger. It coils silently around
fading discipline. Legends say it was once a guardian of focus before
corruption claimed it. Its strike is felt long after it vanishes.

BodyGuard3 - Scale Sentinel An ancient protector clad in impenetrable
emerald scales. It has guarded the Serpent’s throne for centuries
untold. Each scar upon its armor marks a fallen warrior. Only unwavering
resolve can pierce its defense.

BodyGuard4 - Twin-Fang Hydra Spawn Spawned from chaotic surges of
fractured attention, it bears two cunning minds. Each head hisses
different temptations. Sever one distraction, and the other grows
fierce. Its presence fractures clarity.

BodyGuard5 - Coil Reaver The executioner of prolonged indulgence. It
tightens slowly, draining strength through endless pressure. Warriors
who hesitate feel its crushing embrace. Escape demands decisive force.
